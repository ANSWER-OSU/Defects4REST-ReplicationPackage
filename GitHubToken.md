
## **How to Generate a GitHub Token**

**Step 1:** Go to [GitHub.com](https://github.com/)

**Step 2:** Sign in, or sign up if you don’t have an account.

![GitHub Screenshot](/images/github1.png)

**Step 3:** On the dashboard, click your profile icon in the top-right corner.

![GitHub Screenshot](/images/github2.png)

**Step 4:** Click **Settings**.

![GitHub Screenshot](/images/github3.png)

**Step 5:** Scroll down and click **Developer settings**.

![GitHub Screenshot](/images/github4.png)

**Step 6:** Click **Personal access tokens**, then create a new token.

![GitHub Screenshot](/images/github5.png)

**Step 7:** Copy the token (**you won’t be able to see it again**). This token will be used in Step 1.1.
